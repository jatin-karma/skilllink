Getting Started

How It Works

Three simple steps to start mentoring your peers.

01

Create Your Profile

Sign up and fill in your skills, experience level, and a short bio so learners know what to expect from you.

02

Add a Teaching Skill

List the subjects you can teach — Python, Design, Communication, or anything you're confident in. Set your level.

03

Accept & Run Sessions

Learners book sessions with you directly. Accept their requests, run a live session, and earn community ratings.
